About Flavours of Jail

Flavours of Jail started as a tiny experiment — a place for students and coffee lovers to relax and enjoy handcrafted drinks with a twist. We focus on bold coffee, fresh snacks, and a warm vibe that keeps you coming back.

Why visit?

  • Handcrafted espresso-based drinks & cold brews
  • Fresh baked desserts and fast comfort snacks
  • Student discounts & loyalty offer
